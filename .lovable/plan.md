

# Professional Portfolio Website

## Overview
A sleek, dark-themed portfolio website with a modern aesthetic — bold typography, subtle animations, and a CMS-powered backend so you can update your content anytime.

## Design
- **Dark & Modern theme** with a dark background, crisp white text, and accent color highlights
- Smooth scroll animations and hover effects for interactivity
- Fully responsive (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- Clean typography with generous spacing for readability

## Pages & Sections (Single-page layout with smooth scrolling)

### 1. Hero / About Me
- Large intro with your name, title/tagline, and a professional photo
- Brief bio paragraph
- Animated entrance effects

### 2. Projects / Work
- Grid or card-based layout showcasing your work
- Each project card with image, title, description, and tags
- Click to expand for more details or link to external URLs
- Managed via CMS

### 3. Skills & Experience
- Visual skill bars or categorized skill tags
- Timeline or card layout for work experience / education
- CMS-managed

### 4. Blogs & Articles
- Card grid displaying your blog posts and published articles
- Each card with title, short excerpt, date, and a link to the full article (external URL)
- Optional tags/categories for filtering
- CMS-managed — add, edit, or remove posts from the admin dashboard

### 5. Certificates
- Clean grid or list of your certifications and credentials
- Each entry with certificate name, issuing organization, date, and optional link to verify
- Option to display a badge or logo for the issuing organization
- CMS-managed

### 6. Get In Touch
- Contact section with direct links to LinkedIn, email, phone, and other platforms
- Clean icon-based layout — easy ways to reach you

## Backend (Lovable Cloud + Supabase)
- **CMS Dashboard**: A password-protected admin page where you can manage:
  - Projects (title, description, image, tags, links)
  - Skills and experience entries
  - Blog posts & articles (title, excerpt, date, URL, tags)
  - Certificates (name, issuer, date, verification link)
  - Bio, tagline, and contact links
- **Database tables** for projects, skills, experience, blogs, certificates, and site settings
- **Authentication** for the admin dashboard
- **Storage** for uploading project images and certificate badges

## Navigation
- Sticky top navbar with smooth-scroll links to each section
- Subtle highlight on the active section
